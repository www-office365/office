How to Download Microsoft Office

A step-by-step guide for Windows and Mac users

For Windows Users

  1. Visit www.office.com
  2. Click on Sign In and enter your Microsoft account credentials.
  3. Once logged in, click on the Install Office button at the top right corner.
  4. Select Office 365 apps to download the installer.
  5. Open the downloaded setup file (usually named Setup.exe).
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to install Microsoft Office.
  7. Wait for the installation to complete. This may take a few minutes.
  8. Once installed, launch any Office application (like Word or Excel) and activate it using your Microsoft account.

For Mac Users

  1. Go to www.office.com.
  2. Click on Sign In and log in with your Microsoft account.
  3. Click the Install Office button on the dashboard.
  4. Choose the Mac version of Office to download the installer package.
  5. Open the downloaded file (usually with a .pkg extension).
  6. Follow the installation instructions provided by the installer wizard.
  7. Once installed, open any Office app and sign in with your Microsoft account to activate it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to purchase Office to download it?

You can download the installer without a subscription, but activation requires a valid Microsoft 365 subscription or license key.

Can I install Office on multiple devices?

Yes, a Microsoft 365 subscription typically allows installation on multiple devices (PCs, Macs, tablets, and phones).

Is there a free version of Office?

Yes, you can use Office Online for free, which includes limited web-based versions of Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.

What if I forget my Microsoft account password?

Click here to reset your Microsoft account password.

Do I need internet access to use Office?

Internet access is required for downloading and activating Office, but once activated, you can use most features offline.
